Descriptions
Genre:adventure, dark fantasy, martial arts
Demon Slayer is originally from a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows teenage Tanjiro Kamado, who strives to become a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his younger sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. In their journey, they met friends and joined the Demon Slayer Crops.. The 26-episode anime television series was released in October 2020.



Genre:thriller, science fiction, dark fantasy
The promised Neverland is also originally from a Japanese manga series written by Kaiu Shirai and illustrated by Posuka Demizu. The story tells how three gifted kids at an isolated idyllic orphanage discovers the secret and sinister purpose they were raised for. They then tries to look for a way to escape from their evil caretaker and lead the other young children in a risky escape plan.. There are 2 seaons of television series released.



Genre:romance, mecha anime, science fiction
Darling In The Franxx takes place in a dystopian post-apocalyptic future where the remnants of human civilization have abandoned the surface. Artificially-created children, termed "parasites", are deprived of individuality and are educated only for piloting Franxx in pairs to defend humanity. This 24-episode television serie started to released in January, 2018.



Genre:dark fantasy, vampire literature
In Seraph of The End, A young man named Yūichirō Hyakuya joins a vampire extermination unit to avenge the deaths of his orphaned family and reclaim his best friend Mikaela Hyakuya from the vampires. There are 2 seaons of television series released. It is from a manga series written by Takaya Kagami and illustrated by Yamato Yamamoto.



Genre:horror fiction, mystery, tragedy
Another is from a novel also called "Another", by Yukito Ayatsuji.The story focuses on a boy named Kōichi Sakakibara who, upon transferring into Yomiyama Middle School and meeting the curious Mei Misaki, finds himself in a mystery revolving around students and people related to his class falling victim to gruesome, senseless deaths.
